Attractions - Egypt

Favorable Places In Egypt:

The Pyramids

The Red Pyramid Was Egypt's First True (Smooth-Sided) Pyramid. The Three Giza Pyramids Are Aligned. The Pyramid Texts Are the Oldest Known Religious Texts in the World. Climbing the Great Pyramids Is Forbidden. The Pyramids Were Actually White

Nile River

The country Egypt is called the "Gift of the Nile" as it is Egypt's lifeline. Without the Nile, Egypt would have been a desert. Historically, the Nile has provided water for the cultivation of crops in Egypt that led to the burgeoning of many civilizations along the river valley
